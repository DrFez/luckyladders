How to Play Lucky Ladders

Welcome to Lucky Ladders! A game of chance where you climb through levels to win prizes.

Game Rules:

  • Entry Fee: $5 to play
  • Goal: Climb up to 5 levels by rolling dice
  • Winning: At each level, roll equal to or higher than the required number
  • Retry: If you fail a roll (Level 2 or higher), you can pay $2 to retry from the previous level (one retry allowed per game)

Level Requirements:

  • Level 1: Need to roll 2 or higher
  • Level 2: Need to roll 3 or higher
  • Level 3: Need to roll 4 or higher
  • Level 4: Need to roll 5 or higher
  • Level 5: Need to roll 6 (Jackpot!)

Prizes:

  • Level 1: $1
  • Level 2: $3
  • Level 3: $6
  • Level 4: $12
  • Level 5: $30 (Jackpot!)

Strategy: After each successful roll (except Level 5), you can either cash out with your current prize or continue to the next level for a chance at a bigger prize!
